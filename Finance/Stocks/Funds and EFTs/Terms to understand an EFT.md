		- total assets minus total liabilities

- Distribution fee level
	- looks at the fund's expense ratio 
	- distribution fees are also called 12b-1 fees
		- fees paid by funds to cover distribution fees and other shareholder service expenses

- Asset Classes
	- looks at the investment categories in the fund
	- shares, bonds, property, cash

- Expense ratio
	- An expense ratio is a fee expressed as a percentage of a mutual fund or ETF's total assets, which covers the costs of managing the fund, including management fees, administrative expenses, and marketing costs. This fee is deducted from the fund's returns before they are passed on to investors, impacting overall investment performance.

- Turnover
