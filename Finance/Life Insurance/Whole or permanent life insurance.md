- Permanent life insurance provides lifetime coverage with a cash value component that grows tax-deferred.
- The policy allows for borrowing or withdrawals, but this reduces the future death benefit.
- Premiums are higher compared to term [life insurance](https://www.investopedia.com/the-best-life-insurance-companies-8763666), which offers temporary coverage.
- Whole life insurance guarantees steady cash value growth; universal life offers flexible payments and market earnings.
- Converting term life policies to permanent ones is possible without medical exams for flexibility as needs change.
