- Subscriber Identity Module
	- stores information necessary for a phone to connect to a mobile network
		- can a phone still communicate without a SIM card?
			- yes, over wifi or VOIP
	- every sim card as a unique ID number - an ***Internal Mobile Subscriber Identity*** (IMSI) - that is associated with a given user. they also include a ***ICCID (Integrated Circuit Card Identifier)*** serving as a kind of serial number
	- information about the user's data plan is stored on the phone
	- original SIM cards were about the size of a credit card 
	- soft SIM/virtual SIM - cloud based and usually used in tablets and wearables
