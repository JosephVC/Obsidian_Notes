***Bullet size and caliber***
- caliber is the diameter of the gun's barrel and thus the diameter of the round going through it
	- generally measured in mm
	- ![[Pasted image 20260125092039.png]]

- rimfire or centerfire
	- Rimfire cartridges have the primer built into the rim of the casing, while centerfire cartridges have the primer in the center of the rear of the cartridge
	- ![[Pasted image 20260125092349.png]]
		-rimfire cartridges have to be struck on the side of the rim
	- the primer material itself is made of a compound that will ignite upon being struck by a pin, sparking and then igniting the rest of the gunpowder in the round
	- the primer is mainly a brass cup full of [***lead styphnate*** ](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lead_styphnate)which is engineered to explode on impact - this compound is very toxic by itself
	- ![[Pasted image 20260125092445.png]]
- Bullet Grain
	- weight of the bullet and the gunpowder behind it
